Our Founders

Azan

A&A Clothing Store was co-founded by Azan, who met Abylay at Astana IT University. Together, they shared a passion for fashion and started the brand as a hobby, which quickly grew into a recognized name in the industry.

Abylay

Abylay co-founded A&A Clothing Store with Azan, his friend from Astana IT University. Their shared vision and passion for sustainable fashion transformed their hobby into a successful brand.
